Inversion and Rock Property Prediction

OpendTect offers various plugins for quantitative seismic interpretation.

Seismic Colored Inversion (by ARKCLS) is a fast way to convert seismic data to band-limited acoustic impedance.

Full-bandwidth Deterministic and Stochastic Inversion is offered in plugins with the same names by Earthworks and ARKCLS.

Using Neural Networks, it is possible to convert seismic information (e.g. acoustic and/or elastic impedance) to rock properties (e.g. Porosity, Vshale etc). The supervised network is trained along well tracks to find the (non-linear) relationship between seismic and well logs.
