Introduction

OpendTect development is sustained by funding from the industry & governments, maintenance fees collected from commercial users of OpendTect Base (the open source part) and income from commercial plugins.

Madagascar Plugin

Cairn-Energy proposed to link OpendTect to an Open source seismic processing system. We considered a link to SU but our users favored Madagascar.
